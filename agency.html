Agency hosting is specifically designed for web professionals managing multiple client websites. Key differences include:

  • White-label branding: Present the hosting as your own service
  • Centralized management: Control all client sites from one dashboard
  • Client access controls: Granular permissions for clients and team members
  • Bulk actions: Update or manage multiple sites simultaneously
  • Business tools: Client reporting, billing integration, and team collaboration

Regular hosting is designed for individuals or businesses managing their own single website.
