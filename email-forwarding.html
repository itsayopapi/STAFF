What Is Email Forwarding?

mail forwarding is a service that allows you to use custom email addresses with your own domain name, even if you don’t host a mail server or have a dedicated inbox. It lets you automatically redirect incoming messages to any email address of your choice, giving you a professional look without the need for full email hosting.

Why use Email Forwarding

Handling multiple email accounts can be overwhelming. With email forwarding, you can automatically route incoming emails from various addresses to a single inbox, helping you stay organized and responsive. Whether you're a small business, freelancer, or growing enterprise, this feature boosts productivity and simplifies communication across the board.
