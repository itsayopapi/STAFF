Migration FAQs

Answers to common questions about our service

Most standard website migrations are completed within 24-48 hours after we receive all necessary access credentials. The timeline depends on:

  • Website size and complexity
  • Database size
  • Special functionality (eCommerce, memberships, etc.)

Our team will provide a specific timeline after reviewing your setup. We prioritize business-critical sites that can't afford extended downtime.

We implement a zero-downtime migration strategy:

  1. Your site remains fully operational on your current host throughout the process
  2. We test everything on our servers before making any DNS changes
  3. The final DNS update typically results in just 5-15 minutes of downtime during propagation

For mission-critical sites, we can schedule the final switch during your lowest traffic periods.

We typically require:

  • FTP/SFTP access to your current hosting account
  • Database credentials (if applicable)
  • Control panel access (cPanel, Plesk, etc.) if available

For security, we recommend creating temporary credentials that you can revoke after migration. Our team will guide you through this process and never ask for unnecessary access.
