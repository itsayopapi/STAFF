What is Reseller Hosting?

Reseller hosting is a business model that allows you to sell hosting services to your clients while we maintain the servers and infrastructure.

White-Label Solution

Your clients see only your brand - not ours. Custom nameservers, branding, and control panel appearance.

Easy Client Management

Create and manage unlimited hosting accounts from a single dashboard with resource allocation controls.

Recurring Revenue

Generate steady monthly income by hosting client websites with generous profit margins.

Common questions about starting a hosting business

While basic technical knowledge is helpful, our reseller hosting is designed to be user-friendly. We provide:

  • Simple control panel for managing client accounts

  • 24/7 support for you and your clients

All our reseller plans include:

  • White-label hosting under your brand
  • WHM control panel for account management
  • Free SSL certificates for all client sites
  • SSD storage for faster performance
  • LiteSpeed web server with caching
  • 24/7 server monitoring and support

You have several options for billing your clients:

  1. Manual Invoicing: Send invoices directly using PayPal, Stripe, or other payment processors
  2. WHMCS Billing System: Free with our higher plans, automates billing and support tickets

We provide guidance on setting up your preferred billing method.

  • Security concerns
