What Exactly is Reseller Hosting?

It's like renting an apartment building and subleasing the units to tenants.

You purchase hosting resources (server space, bandwidth) in bulk from us at wholesale prices, then package and resell them to your own clients under your brand name.

We handle all the technical server maintenance while you focus on growing your hosting business.
