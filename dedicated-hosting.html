Dedicated hosting provides an entire physical server exclusively for your use, with all resources (CPU, RAM, storage) dedicated to your applications. This offers maximum performance, security, and control compared to shared or VPS hosting solutions.

Dedicated servers are ideal for high-traffic websites, large eCommerce stores, SaaS applications, gaming servers, and businesses with strict security or compliance requirements.
