Domain registration involves selecting a unique domain name and purchasing it through a domain registrar. Once registered, the domain is linked to your website and remains active as long as you renew it before expiration. You can also update DNS settings to connect the domain to your hosting service.

Domain name registration is required to secure a unique web address for your website. It ensures that no one else can use the same domain name and allows users to find your site online. Registration also provides essential ownership details and helps maintain internet security and accountability.

Website hosting is essential because it makes your website accessible online.
