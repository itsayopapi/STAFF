Is WeFitHost suitable for agencies or resellers?

Managed Hosting

Expert management for your hosting needs

What exactly is managed hosting?
Managed hosting means we take care of all the technical aspects of running your server — including setup, maintenance, updates, security, and support — so you can focus on your website and business without worrying about server management.
Who should use managed hosting?
Managed hosting is perfect for businesses, agencies, e-commerce stores, and anyone who wants high performance and expert support without the need to manage the technical side themselves.
What services are included with WeFitHost managed hosting?
Our managed hosting includes server monitoring, regular software updates, security patches, backups, website migrations, performance tuning, and 24/7 expert support.
How is managed hosting different from regular web hosting?
With regular hosting, you're responsible for managing your server and fixing technical issues. With WeFitHost managed hosting, our team handles all of that for you, ensuring your site stays fast, secure, and online — without you lifting a finger.
Will you help if my site goes down or has technical issues?
Yes! Our monitoring systems alert us instantly if there's an issue, and our support team will work proactively to fix it — often before you even notice.
Can I move my existing website to WeFitHost managed hosting?
Definitely. We offer free, hassle-free migrations, including websites, databases, and email accounts, with no downtime during the transfer.
Is WeFitHost managed hosting secure?
Absolutely. We prioritize security with proactive threat monitoring, DDoS protection, firewalls, malware scanning, and regular backups — all included at no extra cost.
Do you offer custom solutions for high-traffic or complex websites?
Yes! We can tailor a managed hosting plan specifically for your needs, including custom server configurations, load balancing, and scaling for high-traffic or resource-intensive sites.
How do I access my hosting account or manage my website?
You'll have access to an intuitive dashboard (like cPanel or a custom WeFitHost panel) where you can manage your website, emails, backups, and more — all with just a few clicks.
What makes WeFitHost managed hosting better than the rest?
We combine true UK-based infrastructure, real human support, powerful technology, and transparent pricing — all designed to give you peace of mind and the best possible hosting experience.

Security Center

Protecting your online presence

How does WeFitHost protect my website?
We use advanced security measures like firewalls, malware scanning, DDoS protection, SSL certificates, and real-time threat monitoring to keep your website safe.
What is DDoS protection and do you provide it?
Yes, we do. DDoS protection helps defend your website from malicious attacks that try to overwhelm your server and take your site offline — keeping your business running smoothly.
Is an SSL certificate included with my hosting plan?
Absolutely. Every WeFitHost plan comes with a free SSL certificate to encrypt data between your site and its visitors, building trust and protecting sensitive information.
How often are backups performed?
We perform daily backups automatically, so your website, databases, and emails are safely stored and can be quickly restored if needed.
Can I manually back up my website whenever I want?
Yes! Through your hosting control panel, you can create manual backups anytime for extra peace of mind.
How do you handle malware or hacks?
If malware or hacking is detected, our security team immediately steps in to clean, restore, and protect your website — often before you even notice a problem.
Where is my website data stored?
All your data is securely stored in UK-based Tier 3 data centers, fully compliant with UK and EU regulations for privacy and data protection.
Do you offer security monitoring services?
Yes. WeFitHost includes continuous monitoring for vulnerabilities, suspicious activity, and unauthorized access attempts as part of our managed hosting services.
How can I make my website even more secure?
We recommend using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, updating plugins and software regularly, and letting us handle server-level protection and security patches.
What happens if my website is compromised?
We're here 24/7 to help. Our support team will investigate, clean up your site, and strengthen your security — quickly and professionally.
