Reseller Hosting FAQs

Common questions about our reseller program

White-label reseller hosting allows you to sell hosting services under your own brand name. All control panels, emails, and client-facing interfaces display your branding instead of ours, making it appear as your own hosting service.

You'll have access to our reseller control panel (WHM) where you can create, modify, and manage all client accounts. Each client gets their own cPanel interface branded with your logo and company information.

Yes, you can upgrade your reseller plan at any time without downtime or migration. We'll automatically adjust your resource limits and prorate any difference in pricing.

We include a free WHMCS license with all reseller plans, which is the industry-standard billing automation platform for hosting businesses. It handles client signups, invoicing, support tickets, and more.

You have two options: 1) Handle all support yourself through your own channels, or 2) Route support requests to our team while maintaining your branding. Many resellers start by handling basic requests themselves and escalating technical issues to us.
