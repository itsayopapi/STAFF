PROCESS

Our Logo Design Process

A collaborative approach to creating your perfect logo

1

Discovery & Research

We start with an in-depth questionnaire and market research to understand your business, target audience, competitors, and design preferences. This helps us establish the creative direction and ensure your logo stands out in your industry.

2

Concept Development

Our designers create multiple initial concepts based on your brand identity. We explore different styles, colors, and typography options to provide you with a diverse range of creative directions to choose from.

3

Client Review

You receive 3-5 initial logo concepts to review in a private online presentation. We gather your detailed feedback to refine the direction before moving forward with the chosen concept.

4

Refinement

We refine the selected concept(s) based on your feedback, making precise adjustments to colors, typography, composition, and any other elements until it perfectly represents your brand vision.

5

Final Delivery

You receive all final logo files in multiple formats (PNG, JPG, SVG, EPS) with various color versions, along with a comprehensive brand style guide for consistent usage across all platforms and media.
