Frequently asked questions

What is a domain name, and why do I need one?

A domain name is your website’s unique address on the internet (e.g., yourwebsite.com). It helps customers find you online and builds your brand identity.

How do I register a domain name?

Simply search for your desired domain on our platform, choose an available name, select a plan, and complete the registration process.

Can I transfer my domain to WeFitHost?

Yes! You can transfer your existing domain to WeFitHost easily. Just ensure your domain is unlocked, and you have the transfer authorization code from your current provider.

WWhat happens if my domain expires?

If your domain expires, it enters a grace period where you can renew it. After this period, it may become available for others to purchase, so timely renewal is crucial.

Do I get free domain privacy protection?

Yes, WeFitHost offers domain privacy protection to keep your personal information safe from spammers and scammers.
