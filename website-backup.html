Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our backup service

How often are backups performed?

Backup frequency depends on your plan. Our Starter plan performs daily backups, Business does twice daily, and Enterprise offers real-time backups. You can also manually trigger backups anytime.

Where are my backups stored?

All backups are stored in secure, geographically redundant data centers separate from your hosting server. You can choose between US, EU, or Asia-Pacific storage locations.

How long are backups kept?

Retention periods vary by plan: Starter keeps backups for 30 days, Business for 90 days, and Enterprise for 365 days. We can accommodate custom retention needs for enterprise clients.

What's included in the backup?

We backup all website files, databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL), emails (on request), and configurations. The backup includes everything needed to fully restore your website.

How quickly can I restore my website?

Most restores complete in under 5 minutes, depending on website size. You can restore your entire site or select individual files/databases through our control panel.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our backup service, we'll refund your payment.
