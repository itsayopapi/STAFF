Our Security in Action

A multi-layered defense system that works around the clock to protect your website

1. Threat Detection

Our AI-powered systems continuously monitor for suspicious activity, malware signatures, and attack patterns using machine learning and global threat intelligence feeds.

2. Immediate Response

When a threat is detected, our systems automatically block malicious traffic, quarantine infected files, and alert our security team for further investigation.

3. Attack Mitigation

For DDoS attacks, traffic is routed through our global scrubbing centers where malicious packets are filtered out before reaching your server, ensuring uninterrupted service.

4. Recovery & Reporting

If any damage occurs, automated backups can restore your site within minutes. Detailed security reports provide insights to help prevent future attacks.
