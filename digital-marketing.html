Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our digital marketing services

SEO is a long-term strategy, and the timeline for seeing results can vary depending on your industry, competition, and the current state of your website. Typically, you may start seeing some improvements in 3-6 months, with more significant results appearing in 6-12 months. However, our clients often report noticing positive changes in their website performance within the first few months of implementing our strategies.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on improving your website's visibility in organic (non-paid) search results through techniques like content optimization, technical improvements, and link building. PPC (Pay-Per-Click) involves paying for ads that appear at the top of search results or on other platforms. The main differences are:

  • SEO: Free traffic (you pay for the service, not clicks), slower to show results, long-term benefits
  • PPC: Immediate traffic (you pay per click), more control over targeting, stops when budget runs out

We recommend using both strategies together for maximum impact.

We use a comprehensive set of KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) tailored to your specific business goals. Some common metrics we track include:

  • For SEO: Organic traffic growth, keyword rankings, click-through rates, domain authority
  • For PPC: Click-through rate (CTR), cost per acquisition (CPA), conversion rate, return on ad spend (ROAS)
  • For Content Marketing: Engagement metrics (time on page, bounce rate), lead generation, social shares

We provide detailed monthly reports with clear insights and actionable recommendations.
