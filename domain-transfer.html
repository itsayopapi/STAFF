How to Transfer Your Domain

Simple steps to move your domain to WeFitHost

Check Domain Eligibility

Verify your domain is eligible for transfer and not within 60 days of registration or previous transfer.

Unlock Your Domain

Log in to your current registrar and disable the domain lock to allow transfers.

Get Authorization Code

Request the EPP/authorization code from your current registrar (also called transfer key).

Initiate Transfer

Enter your domain and authorization code at WeFitHost to start the transfer process.
